**Google Ads Customer Insights Dashboard**
**Project Overview**

This project analyzes customer acquisition and engagement data collected through various digital marketing channels. The objective was to identify the most effective referral sources, understand customer demographics, and uncover trends that can support data-driven marketing decisions.

Using Power BI, an interactive dashboard was developed to provide stakeholders with a comprehensive view of customer acquisition patterns, educational backgrounds, age demographics, and engagement trends across multiple channels.


**Business Problem**

Marketing teams often invest in multiple acquisition channels without clear visibility into which channels generate the highest customer engagement.

The key business questions addressed were:

- Which marketing channels drive the highest customer acquisition?
- What are the demographic characteristics of acquired customers?
- How does customer engagement vary by gender over time?
- What educational backgrounds are most common among customers?
- How can marketing spend be optimized based on channel performance?

**Dataset Summary**

The dataset contains customer acquisition information collected through various referral channels, including:

- Facebook Ads
- Instagram Ads
- Google Ads
- Google Search
- YouTube
- YouTube Ads
- Banner Ads
- Company Website
- Friend Referrals
- Other Sources


**Tools & Technologies**
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modelling
- Data Visualization
-Business Intelligence

**Key Insight**
1. Facebook Ads generated approximately 33% of all customer acquisitions, making it the most effective marketing channel. Instagram Ads contributed over 22%, while word-of-mouth referrals accounted for 17% of total customers.

2. The largest customer segments were: Customers aged 25–44 represent the core audience, accounting for the majority of customer acquisitions. Marketing campaigns can therefore be optimized toward this demographic for higher ROI.

3. Education Analysis Customer qualification levels were examined to understand the educational profile of acquired users. More than 77% of customers possess undergraduate or postgraduate qualifications, indicating that marketing content resonates strongly with highly educated audiences.

4. Gender Engagement Trends
Female engagement demonstrated stronger growth trends.
Engagement declined after 2024, suggesting a need for campaign optimization and retention strategies.

**Business Recommendations**
- Marketing Optimization
- Increase investment in Facebook and Instagram advertising, which contribute the majority of customer acquisitions.
- Expand referral programs to capitalize on strong word-of-mouth performance.

Audience Targeting
- Focus campaign messaging on professionals aged 25–44.
- Create tailored content for degree-holding audiences, who represent the largest customer segment.

Retention Strategy
- Investigate factors contributing to the post-2024 engagement decline.
- Develop re-engagement campaigns targeting previously active customer groups.

Channel Diversification
- Evaluate underperforming channels such as Banner Ads and Company Website referrals.
- Reallocate budget toward higher-performing acquisition sources.

**Key Outcomes**
- Identified Facebook Ads as the highest-performing acquisition channel.
- Revealed that customers aged 25–44 form the primary target audience.
- Demonstrated that over three-quarters of customers hold undergraduate or postgraduate qualifications.
- Enabled data-driven decision-making for marketing budget allocation.
- Delivered an interactive dashboard providing real-time customer acquisition insights.
